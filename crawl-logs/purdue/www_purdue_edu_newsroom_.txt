News Service staff write and place news about Purdue people, events and research in local, national and global media. The News Service also connects Purdue experts with reporters and the outside world. The office promotes engagement by helping businesses and communities tap into Purdue resources that can improve quality of life. News Service offers consultation and media relations training for Purdue faculty, staff and administrators. The staff also works with campus safety organizations to develop and implement crisis communication plans.
