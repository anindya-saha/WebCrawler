As a high school freshman, she led a drive to gather soap, towels and other toiletries for her local homeless shelter. At Purdue, Jazna who is studying public relations took her project to the next level by collaborating with Alpha Kappa Psi business fraternity. "Most people would say the group that benefits the most are the residents of the shelters, but I would say it's the shelters themselves since we provide tens of thousands of dollars in donations," Jazna says.
