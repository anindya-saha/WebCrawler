Students enrolled in undergraduate institutions around the world spend the fall or spring semester at DKU and engage in interdisciplinary classes in global health; the humanities; physical, biological, and social sciences; and Chinese language, all taught by Duke faculty members. Learn More About the Undergraduate Semester Master of Science in Global Health The DKU Master of Science in Global Health program is a new approach to learning about ways to prevent disease; the social, economic, political and environmental causes of disease; and effective strategies to increase health equity among the world’s rich and poor. Learn More About the Global Health Program Master of Science in Medical Physics The DKU Master of Science in Medical Physics program provides the educational foundation and basic clinical knowledge needed for a career in medical physics. The program prepares graduates for employment in a variety of settings including academia, industry, clinical services, and government facilities. Learn More About the Medical Physics Program Master of Management Studies (MMS) The ten-month Master of Management Studies: DKU program provides recent college graduates immersive learning experiences in the United States and China. Students learn concepts from each of the functional areas of business as they prepare to build their careers. Learn More About the MMS Program Contact Us Today to Learn More.
