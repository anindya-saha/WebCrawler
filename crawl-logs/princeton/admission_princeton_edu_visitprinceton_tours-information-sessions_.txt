Weekend information sessions and tours  will meet at the Frist Campus Center. For directions to Clio Hall and the Frist Campus Center, print this  campus map for Admission visitors. Tours of the School of Engineering and Applied Science  are available for prospective students on weekdays when classes are in session. For more information, please visit the  school's website  for tour schedules. For information on visiting campus, see Princeton’s  travel page . Visitors driving to campus on weekdays may park in Lot 23 located at the south end of campus and take the Tiger Transit  shuttle  (the Orange Line) to Clio Hall. Please note we are unable to store personal items such as luggage, backpacks, coats, or umbrellas at Clio Hall or the Frist Campus Center. If you have any questions, please call the Admission Office at (609) 258-3060.
